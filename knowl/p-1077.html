A periodic square wave function \(f(t)\) in terms of unit step function is given as
\begin{equation*} f(t) =k[u_{o}(t)-2u_{a}(t)+2u_{2a}(t)-2u_{3a}(t)+\cdots]. \end{equation*}
Show that the Laplce Transform of \(f(t)\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \mathscr{L}\{f(t)\}=\frac{k}{s}\tanh (\frac{as}{2}). \end{equation*}
