If there is no heat flow in \(z\)- direction (or temperature of the body is independent of \(z\)), then
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial z^{2}}=0 \end{equation*}
and heat equation
\begin{equation} \frac{1}{h^{2}} \frac{\partial u}{\partial t}= \frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x^{2}}+\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial y^{2}} \tag{7.3.7} \end{equation}
is known as heat equation for two - dimensional flow parallel to \(xy\) plane.
