We know that
\begin{equation*} xJ'_{n}=nJ_{n}-xJ_{n+1} \end{equation*}
[from relation 1.]
\begin{equation*} xJ'_{n}=-nJ_{n}+xJ_{n-1} \end{equation*}
[from relation 2.] Adding these, we get -
\begin{equation*} 2xJ'_{n}= -xJ_{n+1}+xJ_{n-1} \end{equation*}
or,
\begin{equation*} 2J'_{n} =J_{n-1}-J_{n+1} \end{equation*}
