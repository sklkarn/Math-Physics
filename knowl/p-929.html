\begin{equation*} f(x) = \begin{cases} 1, & |x| \lt a \\ 0, & |x| \gt a \end{cases} \end{equation*}
and hence evalute
  1. \begin{equation*} \int\limits_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{\sin sa.\cos sa}{s}\,ds \end{equation*}
