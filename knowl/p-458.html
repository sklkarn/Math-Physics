Assume
  1. \(y_{1}=e^{mx}\) be a known integral of C.F. if \(m^{2}+mP+Q=0\text{.}\)
  2. \(y_{1}=e^{x}\) be a known integral of C.F. if \(1+P+Q=0 \text{.}\)
  3. \(y_{1}=e^{-x} \) be a known integral of C.F. if \(1-P+Q=0\text{.}\)
  4. \(y_{1}=x \) be a known integral of C.F. if \(P+Qx=0\text{.}\)
  5. \(y_{1}=x^{2}\) be a known integral of C.F. if \(2+2Px+Qx^{2}=0 \text{.}\)
  6. and put \(y=vy_{1}\) be the solution of corresponding homogeneous differential equation. Where \(v\) is any parameter depends on \(x\text{.}\)
