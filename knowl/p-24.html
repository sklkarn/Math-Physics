If these vectors are linearly dependent then \(l\vec{a} + m\vec{b}+ n\vec{c} =0\text{.}\) where \(l, m, n\) are scalars, and at least one scalar being non-zero, i.e.,
\begin{align*} l( \hat{i}-3\hat{j}+2\hat{k}) + m(2\hat{i}-4\hat{j}-\hat{k}) + n(3\hat{i}+2\hat{j}-\hat{k}) \amp = 0\\ \text{or,} \quad (l+2m+3n)\hat{i} + (-3l-4m+2n)\hat{j} + (2l-m-n)\hat{k}\amp =0 \end{align*}
