The scalar or dot product of two vectors \(\vec{a}\text{,}\) and \(\vec{b}\) is denoted by \(\vec{a}. \vec{b}\) and is defined as
\begin{equation*} \vec{a}\cdot {\vec{b}} = |\vec{a}|. |\vec{b}| \cos\theta= ab\cos\theta\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(\theta\) is the angle between \(\vec{a}\) and \(\vec{b}\text{.}\)
