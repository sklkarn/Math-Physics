We know that -
\begin{equation*} xJ'_{n}=nJ_{n}-xJ_{n+1} \end{equation*}
[from relation 1.]
\begin{equation*} xJ'_{n}=-nJ_{n}+xJ_{n-1} \end{equation*}
[from relation 2.] Subtracting these, we get -
\begin{equation*} 0= 2nJ_{n}-xJ_{n+1}-xJ_{n-1} \end{equation*}
or,
\begin{equation*} 2nJ_{n} =x(J_{n-1}+J_{n+1}) \end{equation*}
