Item c.

Useful Results: If \(\hat{i}\text{,}\) \(\hat{j}\text{,}\) \(\hat{k}\text{,}\) be the unit vector along \(x\text{,}\) \(y\text{,}\) \(z\) axes, respectively, then
\begin{align*} \hat{i}\cdot{\hat{i}} \amp = \hat{j}\cdot{\hat{j}} = \hat{k}\cdot{\hat{k}} =1 \hspace{1cm} \because \theta = 0^{o}\\ \hat{i}\cdot{\hat{j}} \amp = \hat{j}\cdot{\hat{k}} = \hat{k}\cdot{\hat{i}} = 0 \hspace{1cm} \because \theta = 90^{o} \end{align*}
