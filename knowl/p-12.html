Let a vector \(\vec{a}\) is multiplied by any real positive number \(n\text{,}\) then the product \(n \vec{a}\) is a vector whose magnitude is \(n\) times as that of \(\vec{a}\) and its direction is the same as \(\vec{a}\text{.}\) If \(n\) is a negative number then the direction of \(n \vec{a}\) is opposite to \(\vec{a}\text{.}\)
